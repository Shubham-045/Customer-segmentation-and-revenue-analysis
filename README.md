# Customer Segmentation Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer data to understand income distribution, purchasing behavior, and spending patterns. An interactive Power BI dashboard is developed to segment customers based on various attributes such as age group, income group, and preferred category.

The objective is to convert raw customer data into meaningful insights that help businesses improve customer targeting and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer income and spending behavior  
- Understand purchase frequency across product categories  
- Segment customers based on income group and demographics  
- Identify patterns across different age groups  
- Evaluate customer preferences for different product categories  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Income:** 89M  
- **Total Customers:** 1000  
- **Total Last Purchase Amount:** 492.35K  
- **Total Spending Score:** 51K  

---

## 🖥 Dashboard Features

### Customer Behavior Analysis:
- Purchase frequency by preferred category  
- Last purchase amount by age group  
- Spending score distribution across age groups  

### Demographic Insights:
- Income distribution by income group (High, Medium, Low)  
- Customer distribution by gender and preferred category  
- Membership years analysis by age group  

### Category Analysis:
- Preferred product categories (Electronics, Sports, Groceries, etc.)  
- Category-wise customer distribution  

### Filters:
- Gender (Male, Female, Other)  
- Income Segments (High, Medium, Low)  
- Preferred Category  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI  
- DAX (for calculated measures)  
- Data Modeling  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Majority of revenue comes from high-income customer group  
- Customers in age group **20–30** show higher income and spending patterns  
- Electronics and Sports categories have higher purchase frequency  
- Spending score decreases slightly with increasing age  
- Customer distribution is relatively balanced across categories

## 🚀 Business Impact
This dashboard helps businesses:
- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Improve targeted marketing strategies  
- Optimize product offerings based on customer preferences  
- Understand demographic-based purchasing behavior  
- Enhance customer retention strategies
